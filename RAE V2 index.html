What We Do
Our Services

Complete auto electrical solutions for all commercial vehicles — delivered with 30+ years of hands-on expertise.

Truck & Commercial Vehicle Repair

Full electrical diagnosis and repair for heavy-duty trucks, lorries, and all types of commercial transport vehicles.

Bus Electrical Services

Comprehensive electrical repairs and maintenance for passenger buses including lighting systems, dashboard wiring, and full electrical diagnostics.

Crane Electrical Systems

Specialized electrical solutions for cranes and heavy lifting equipment — safe, reliable, and built to last.

Vehicle Lighting Work

LED headlight upgrades, indicator systems, interior lighting, and full lighting circuit repairs for all vehicles.

Electrical Wiring Services

Complete rewiring, harness replacement, and custom wiring solutions for commercial and heavy vehicles.

Battery Services

Battery testing, charging, replacement, and installation for trucks, buses, and heavy commercial vehicles.
